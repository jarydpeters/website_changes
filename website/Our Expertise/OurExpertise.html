Our Expertise

DISTek specializes in cutting-edge software development services. Our engineers are experts in vehicle simulator design, automated test equipment, and embedded software. With broad expertise across areas like vehicle simulator design, ECU software development, and vehicle networking, we support innovation across a wide range of industries—from off-highway vehicles to infrastructure technology and beyond.1

Vehicle Simulation

We specialize in virtual testing systems and implementation of model-based software development (MBSD) solutions for a wide variety of commercial vehicle and off-highway equipment applications.

Embedded Software

Our expert teams use Agile methodologies to produce high-quality software for vehicle embedded systems in order to collaborate with our clients to maintain project priority.

Vehicle Networking

Our engineers draw from decades of experience developing efficient and scalable solutions for both standards-based and proprietary vehicle networks.
